Package Design Project


“My phone is dead.” How many times have we said or heard that phrase? Well, if that’s the case, give it a proper burial. The Undead Charger is a coffin for your phone, resurrection (charging) station, and altogether comical tech accessory. I created this package design as part of a project at Seattle University in 2015 and would love to see it in production someday. You saw it here first.
